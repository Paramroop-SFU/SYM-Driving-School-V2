About us

SYM Driving School

The owner of SYM Driving School started established this driving school more than 12 years ago. He was an experienced truck driver who has decades of experience driving different types of vehicles. The owner and many of his friends are all experienced drivers, so he decided to start up a driving school with his friends.

All the coaches at SYM Driving School are class 1 drivers with 30+ years of driving experience and have taught at SYM Driving School for over a decade.

Coaches at SYM Driving School had helped over thousands of students pass their class 5/7 road test with flying colours in their decade of service.
