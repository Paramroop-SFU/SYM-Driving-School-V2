About us

SYM Driving School

SYM Driving School was established in 2012. The owner is an experienced driver with a decades-long track record of safe and accident-free driving. The school is committed to passing on years of driving experience and knowledge to a new generation of drivers with numerous excellent qualified instructors with more than 30 years of safe driving experience.

At SYM Driving School, you will not only learn to drive safely, you can also about learn traffic laws, traffic signs and many other theoretical knowledge with driving.

Since the establishment of the school, coaches at SYM Driving School had helped over thousands of students pass their class 4, class 5, and class 7 road test with flying colours.
