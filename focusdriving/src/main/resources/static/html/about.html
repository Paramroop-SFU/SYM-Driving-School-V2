About us

SYM Driving School

Simon Chow started SYM Driving School more than 12 years ago. He was a experienced truck driver who has decades of experience driving different types of vehicles. Simon and many of his friends are all experienced drivers, so he decided to start up a driving school with his friends.

All the coaches at SYM Driving School are class 1 drivers with 30+ years of driving experience and have taught at SYM Driving School for over a decade.

Simon and other fellow coaches at SYM Driving School had helped over thousands of students pass their class 5/7 road test with flying colours in their decade of service.
