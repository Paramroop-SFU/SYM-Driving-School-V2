Services Offered

Below are few of the services we provide:

Regular Driving Lessons

60 minute of lesson time. Rates fluctuates and include pick up and drop off. Please contact for more info

Car Rentals for Road Tests

Car rental for road test includes a 60 minute warm-up before road test. If customer is not a student of SYM Driving School, an 1 hour driving skill assessment will be required. Rates are the same as Regular Driving Lessons. Please contact for more info

Road Test Booking Service

SYM Driving School can book your road test for you (you need your own vehicle)

Additional Passenger

We allow 1 additional passenger to observe the lesson. Please contact for more info


Additional Info:

There will be no refunds for lessons that have taken place, unused lessons balance will be refunded upon request. Please email us at SYMCHOW@gmail.com for refund requests.

Lesson cancellations must be done atleast 24-hours in advanced to avoid being charged 50% of the lesson fee as cancellation fee. Cancellation must be done by e-mail, phone or text. If a student is absent for a booked lesson, they will be charged the full amount for that lesson.

Road test cancellations must be done atleast 72-hours in advance for us to alert ICBC. Failure to give 72-hour notice in advance will result in a late cancellation fee charged by ICBC.

SYM Driving School reserve the right to claim against the student who cause an accident during a lesson or road test.
